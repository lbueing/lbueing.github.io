Leah Bueing

Coding

Alone at my desk I sit and hit keys

"This is easy!" I muse, "such a warm autumn's breeze!"

Content as I am, all the joy I possess

Creating new lines, while the code I assess

Fine it is going until my dismay

Abug has appeared, planning to ruin my day!

"I'll find you!" I yell, "you bugger, you worm!"

Frantic my heart, as my insides do squirm

Where can it be? My methods are sound...

I've checked my classes to pieces, it cannot be found!

Shelving my pride, I seek a new eye

"Can you find my mistake?" I say with a sigh

"Hmm...I can try, but I make no guarantee.

Ah yes, here we are, factor is missing its 'c'."

Then right on cue, my palm hits my face

As I trudge to my desk, to put the c in its place.
